About Me

The Developer Behind the Reels

L

Lokendra  ·  Slot Game Developer

I turn reel mechanics into unforgettable player experiences — one frame at a time.

With 3.5 years deep in the iGaming trenches, I specialise in building slot games that feel alive. My tools of choice are Pixi.js and TypeScript — but what really drives my work is obsessing over the micro-details: the weight of a reel stop, the timing of a win celebration, the fluidity of a symbol cascade.

I've contributed to games spanning classic Vegas titles to complex Megaways engines. I understand both the player psychology behind good slot design and the technical architecture that makes it run at 60 fps across every device.
